Lasagna

Description

The most incredible lasagna you will ever taste. Made with the freshes roma tomatoes, bechamel sauce and fresh mozzarela

This lasagna is so good and tasteful that it will leave all your guests begging for the recipe, get ready to be the center of the show at your dinner party.

Ingredients!

  • 10 sheets of fresh lasagna, store bought
  • olive oil
  • 2 medium red onions minced
  • 1 garlic bulb minced
  • 4 oz of bacon, cut into cubes
  • 8 fl oz of dry red wine
  • 24 oz of crushed canned roma tomatoes
  • 8 oz ground beef
  • 4 oz of pecorino cheese
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 8 oz Ground sausage
  • 16 oz of mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Tomato Sauce

Start by sweating your minced onions until golden brown and than add the garlic. Once both are sweated, at the ground beef, bacon and the ground sauce sear until golden bronw. Add the red wine and reduce by half. Finally add the can of tomatoes and simmer for 2 hours.

Assemble

You'll alternate layers of rich, meaty sauce, with pancetta ground beef and Italian sausage, silky, fresh lasagna noodles, and a creamy blend of cottage cheese, pecorino Romano and mozzarella cheese. A final layer of melted mozzarella brings the dish home.

Bon Apetito!
